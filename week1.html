Week 1 - Introduction to Employability Skills Development

Session Details

Date: 1st Feb 2025

Resource Person: Ms. Vishaliney Pirathap

Designation: Lecturer, Northern Uni

Topic: Introduction to Employability Skills Development

Topic Summary

In this foundational session, Ms. Vishaliney Pirathap introduced the Employability Skills Development (ESD) module, outlining its objectives and structure. She highlighted the critical role of employability skills in standing out in today’s competitive job market, emphasizing their importance for career success.

What Was Taught

Ms. Pirathap explained that employability skills, such as effective communication, teamwork, time management, and problem-solving, are essential for professional growth. She discussed how these skills complement technical expertise and are highly valued by employers. The session included an overview of the module’s weekly structure and expectations, encouraging students to engage in self-assessments and group activities to develop these skills.

What Was Learned

I gained a deeper understanding of how employability skills can enhance my career prospects. The session clarified the importance of proactively developing soft skills early in my academic journey to prepare for professional environments. I also learned the value of self-reflection in identifying areas for improvement.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

Moving forward, I will actively participate in group discussions and collaborative projects to strengthen my teamwork and communication skills. I’ll also use self-assessment tools to track my progress and identify gaps in my employability skills, aiming to build a strong foundation for my career.
