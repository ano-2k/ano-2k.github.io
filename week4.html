Week 4 - Industry Expectations, Skills, and Attitudes – Technical Aspect

Session Details

Date: 22nd Feb 2025

Resource Person: Mr. Miller Alexander Rajendran

Designation: CEO, SenzAgro Solutions

Topic: Industry Expectations, Skills, and Attitudes – Technical Aspect

Topic Summary

  • Mr. Miller Alexander Rajendran shared practical advice on navigating the corporate world.
  • He focused on effectively presenting technical skills and achievements to stand out in a competitive environment.

What Was Taught

  • Mr. Miller emphasized the importance of visibility in the workplace, warning that failing to showcase one’s work could allow others to take credit.
  • He encouraged confidence, clear communication, and taking ownership of accomplishments.
  • While technical skills are crucial, attitude and proactive communication are equally important for career advancement.
  • He discussed strategies for documenting and presenting achievements effectively.

What Was Learned

  • Technical expertise alone is not enough; I need to actively communicate my contributions to gain recognition.
  • The session taught the value of confidence and assertiveness in professional settings.
  • I learned the need to document my work systematically to showcase my skills.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

  • I will document my project contributions in a structured manner, such as through reports or presentations, to highlight my achievements.
  • I’ll practice speaking up in team settings to ensure my work is recognized.
  • I aim to build confidence in presenting my skills during internships and job interviews.
