Week 4 - Industry Expectations, Skills, and Attitudes – Technical Aspect

Session Details

Date: 22nd Feb 2025

Resource Person: Mr. Miller Alexander Rajendran

Designation: CEO, SenzAgro Solutions

Topic: Industry Expectations, Skills, and Attitudes – Technical Aspect

Topic Summary

Mr. Miller Alexander Rajendran shared practical advice on navigating the corporate world, focusing on how to effectively present technical skills and achievements to stand out in a competitive environment.

What Was Taught

Mr. Miller emphasized the importance of visibility in the workplace, warning that failing to showcase one’s work could allow others to take credit. He encouraged confidence, clear communication, and taking ownership of accomplishments. The session highlighted that while technical skills are crucial, attitude and proactive communication are equally important for career advancement. He also discussed strategies for documenting and presenting achievements effectively.

What Was Learned

I realized that technical expertise alone is not enough; I need to actively communicate my contributions to gain recognition. The session taught me the value of confidence and assertiveness in professional settings, as well as the need to document my work systematically to showcase my skills.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

I will start documenting my project contributions in a structured manner, such as through reports or presentations, to highlight my achievements. I’ll also practice speaking up in team settings to ensure my work is recognized. In the long term, I aim to build confidence in presenting my skills during internships and job interviews.
