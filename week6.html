Week 6 - Skills Required to Select a Career Path

Session Details

Date: 22nd Mar 2025

Resource Person: Mr. Gajarthan Thevarajah

Designation: Co-Founder & CEO, Bohar Solutions Pvt Ltd

Topic: Skills Required to Select a Career Path

Topic Summary

Mr. Gajarthan Thevarajah shared his inspiring entrepreneurial journey, offering practical advice on choosing a career path that aligns with personal passion and market demands. He emphasized starting small, building momentum through projects, and learning by doing to carve a successful career path.

What Was Taught

Mr. Gajarthan emphasized the importance of self-awareness in identifying one’s strengths and passions to guide career choices. He discussed the need for self-learning, adaptability, and resilience in navigating career challenges, encouraging students to take calculated risks and continuously improve skills to stay competitive. His personal story highlighted that career paths are often non-linear, requiring persistence and openness to new opportunities. He stressed the importance of starting projects early, even if imperfect, and shared the tip: "You don't need to learn everything - just start building with what you know." The session also covered technical skills like Git, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and UI/UX design with Figma, alongside soft skills like communication, consistency, and curiosity. Common mistakes to avoid included focusing too much on theory, not asking for help, and delaying sharing work publicly.

What Was Learned

I learned that choosing a career involves balancing personal interests with market needs, and that resilience and curiosity are key to overcoming obstacles and seizing opportunities. The session taught me the value of starting messy and building projects to showcase skills, rather than waiting for perfection. I also understood the importance of using platforms like GitHub to document work and connecting with mentors for guidance. Continuous learning and taking action early are essential for long-term career success.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

I will reflect on my passions and strengths to identify potential career paths and explore new areas of interest through online courses and side projects to broaden my skill set. I plan to take calculated risks, such as pursuing internships in diverse fields, to discover the best fit for my career goals. Additionally, I will build a personal website, push at least 10 commits to GitHub, and join or start a student developer group to collaborate and learn. I’ll also post weekly updates on LinkedIn to share my progress and build my professional presence. In the long term, I aim to execute projects consistently, starting small and improving iteratively.
