Week 8 - Achieving Your Dream – Job Roles in Non-IT Fields

Session Details

Date: 19th Apr 2025

Resource Person: Ms. Sarmila Sivaraja

Designation: Google Women Techmakers Ambassador, CEO – 2SF Labs

Topic: Achieving Your Dream – Job Roles in Non-IT Fields

Topic Summary

  • Ms. Sarmila Sivaraja shared her journey of founding 2SF Labs, a collaborative space for innovation.
  • She provided insights into pursuing dream careers, including opportunities in non-IT fields.

What Was Taught

  • Platforms like Toastmasters improve public speaking, Clubhouse enhances networking, and Coursera and Google I/O support skill development.
  • Knowledge sharing, continuous learning, and reading influential books like “Rich Dad, Poor Dad” are essential.
  • Her story of turning her vision into a startup highlighted perseverance and innovation.
  • Diverse roles in non-IT fields include project management and entrepreneurship.

What Was Learned

  • Ms. Sarmila’s entrepreneurial journey inspired me to explore careers beyond IT.
  • Public speaking, networking, and lifelong learning are critical for achieving professional goals.
  • I gained insights into the mindset required to start a venture.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

  • I will join Toastmasters to improve my public speaking skills.
  • I’ll explore networking opportunities on Clubhouse.
  • I’ll enroll in relevant Coursera courses to broaden my knowledge.
  • I plan to explore entrepreneurial ideas and consider non-IT career paths that align with my interests.
