Week 9 - Business Law and Company Law

Session Details

Date: 6th May 2025

Resource Person: Ms. Vaishnavy Shanmuganathan

Designation: Attorney-at-Law

Topic: Business Law and Company Law

Topic Summary

  • Ms. Vaishnavy Shanmuganathan provided a comprehensive introduction to business and company law.
  • She focused on legal frameworks governing organizations and their relevance to aspiring entrepreneurs.

What Was Taught

  • Legal structures of businesses include sole proprietorship, partnership, and corporation.
  • Intellectual property rights protect business ideas and assets.
  • Contract law ensures clear agreements between parties.
  • Regulatory compliance is crucial for smooth business operations.
  • Real-world examples of legal challenges faced by startups were discussed, along with solutions.

What Was Learned

  • I gained a foundational understanding of business and company law.
  • Choosing the right legal structure is important for a business’s success.
  • Contracts and intellectual property play key roles in protecting ideas and assets.
  • Legal literacy is essential for entrepreneurship.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

  • I will deepen my knowledge of business law through online courses and reading.
  • I’ll consult legal resources for projects involving intellectual property or contracts.
  • I aim to apply legal knowledge to ensure compliance and protect future business endeavors.
