Week 9 - Business Law and Company Law

Session Details

Date: 6th May 2025

Resource Person: Ms. Vaishnavy Shanmuganathan

Designation: Attorney-at-Law

Topic: Business Law and Company Law

Topic Summary

Ms. Vaishnavy Shanmuganathan provided a comprehensive introduction to business and company law, focusing on the legal frameworks that govern organizations and their relevance to aspiring entrepreneurs.

What Was Taught

The session covered the legal structures of businesses (e.g., sole proprietorship, partnership, corporation), intellectual property rights, contract law, and the importance of regulatory compliance. Ms. Vaishnavy explained how legal knowledge is crucial for protecting business interests and ensuring smooth operations. She also discussed real-world examples of legal challenges faced by startups and how to address them.

What Was Learned

I gained a foundational understanding of business and company law, including the importance of choosing the right legal structure for a business. The session highlighted the role of contracts and intellectual property in protecting ideas and assets. I now appreciate the need for legal literacy in entrepreneurship.

What I Plan to Implement in the Future

I will deepen my knowledge of business law through online courses and reading to prepare for potential entrepreneurial ventures. I’ll also consult legal resources when working on projects that involve intellectual property or contracts. In the long term, I aim to apply this knowledge to ensure compliance and protect my future business endeavors.
